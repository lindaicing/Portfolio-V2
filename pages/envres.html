Overview

  • What: Building a repository for resources on where to start learning about different environmentally-related concepts.
  • When: 2021 - Ongoing
  • Tools: Figma, HTML, CSS, JS (vanilla, JQuery)
View the Resource

Problem

The project was started because I wanted to be more aware of different environmentally-related concepts. The goal was to make a resource website that could easily be tabbed-through to navigate different topic areas related to environmentalism.

Who

  • Primary Demographic: People who want to be more environmentally conscious politically, personally, and socially.

Solution

This resource was developed for people who want to learn more about environmentally-related laws and regulations, environmental politics, notable people and organizations, and how to be more conscious in their everyday lives.

Process

Topic Breakdown

  • Home - Homepage.
  • Definitions - Terms relating to environmental sectors, Earth's structure, and acronyms.
  • Legislation in US - Key environmental legislation in the US.
  • Legislation International - Key environmental legislation worldwide.
  • Organizations - Important environmental organizations. Governmental, nonprofit, activist, and conferences.
  • Timeline - Timeline of environmental happenings.
  • Reading Nook - Informative books, articles, and websites to read.
  • Video Corner - Informative videos to watch.
  • Podcasts - Fascinating podcasts discussing environmental matters.
  • Projects - Neat projects for alternative energy, biomaterials, anticonsumption, etc.
  • People - Impactful and influential people, for better or for worse.
  • Lifestyle - Aspects to consider when trying to live more environmentally consciously.
