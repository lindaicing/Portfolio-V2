Overview

Ethicli suggests better products while you shop by scoring how the product affects our workers, planet, and community. Use our free browser extension to effortlessly shop from more ethical, green, and smaller businesses. The site is coded using HTML/CSS and JavaScript (vanilla and JQuery). I coded the visual extension using HTML/CSS/JS, and my teammates built the database in Java. I was the UI/UX, brand, and graphic designer from its conception in 2020, to its conclusion in 2021.

The descriptive poster was developed to explain the overview, purpose, target demographics, competitor differentiation, functionality and next steps for Ethicli. Logo and Imagery

Brainstorming Ethicli's Logo. A few drafts were made, and self-reporting target audience members were asked to provide logo preferences as well. The resulting logo is a hybridization of the "E" of Ethicli, two humans (one in the negative space, and one formed by the "E"), and a heart.
