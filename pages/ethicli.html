Overview

  • What: Ethicli suggests better products while you shop by scoring how the product affects our workers, planet, and community. Use our free browser extension to effortlessly shop from more ethical, green, and smaller businesses.
  • When: May 2020 - Jul 2021
  • Tools: Web, Browser Extension, and Graphic design and development. HTML, CSS, JS (vanilla and JQuery)

Poster

The descriptive poster was developed to explain the overview, problem and purpose, target demographics, competitor differentiation, functionality and next steps for Ethicli. We started with persona development, but had easy access to interview many individuals within the target demographic. The 5 selected subjects in the "Target Market" section are real people who provided feedback regarding the problems they have with ethical shopping, and below their quotes are how Ethicli addresses those concerns.

Extension

The extension was built to have an overall shop rating, rating breakdowns (for Environmental, Labor, Animal Rights, and Social Involvement categories) any badges pertaining to the shop's ethical practices, a burger menu that users can report issues on, and a sponsor portion in which companies with a higher ethical rating than the current shop can display product ads.

Website

Feedback was taken from our users through Google Surveys which inquired about specific experience feedback for buttons, tab-switching, legibility, and usability. Unfortunately the website is no longer live, but archived versions can still be viewed. The website was first developed in Figma, and then coded using HTML/CSS/JS. The redesign of the website was proposed to be lighter in color with subtle animations.

Infopage

The infopage opens when a user wants to get more information about a specific company. Included in the infopage is everything shown on the extension, along with specific article/website sources (with credibility ratings from Media Bias Factchecker) that contribute to each score category (ex. Environmental, Animal Rights, etc.). If the company does not have score data for a specific category, the category score is hidden on the infopage and omitted from the overall score.

Search

The search function was a proposal to be part of the overall website. Results for companies relating to the search would populate below a centered searchbar, and each result would showcase its company along with the ethical rating. Each result would also be able to be clickable, and takes the user to the company's infopage.
