Who

  • Primary Demographic: Middle-aged to older people with established income, who want to help out their aging family members.
  • Secondary Demographic: Local business interested in further establishing online delivery systems. In this project we focused on the primary demographic and will consider the secondary demographic during next steps.

We created and sent out a Google survey with over 75 responses that researched eating habits during COVID.

  • 94% of people get their food from going in person to the grocery store
  • 37% of people use meal delivery services such as Uber Eats, Postmates, Grubhub
  • 5.7% of people use meal delivery kits such as Blue Apron, Hello Fresh
  • 38.9% of people get restaurant delivered meals at least weekly
  • 95.7% laptop/desktop users

Solution

We planned to create a web application that both seniors and their younger loved ones can use, to plan the meal delivery and to really show their seniors they care.
