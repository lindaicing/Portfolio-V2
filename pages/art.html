Kingdom of the Silver Cat Book Cover

Illustrated cover for Tom Carroll’s children’s novel, Kingdom of the Silver Cat, with a reading audience for children grades 4-12. The project includes the cover development and iterations.

Phobias Concentration

The goal of this concentration was to evoke an emotional response of disgust or fear in the viewer. The point of these pieces was not only to aesthetically please but to provoke the audience, to juxtapose humor and lighthearted puns with the irrational aversion of otherwise harmless topics, and to raise awareness of the very real, yet unrealistic, fears that many individuals have. Since not everyone has the phobias represented in this concentration, disgust is instead used to make the viewer feel aversion towards the subject matter.
